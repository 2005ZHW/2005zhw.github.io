[USACO04OPEN] MooFest G

2024-01-27
题解>USACO
4.1k 词
  • CDQ分治

题目描述

DESCRIPTION

Every year, Farmer John’s NNN (1≤N≤20,0001 \leq N \leq 20,0001≤N≤20,000) cows attend “MooFest”,a social gathering of cows from around the world. MooFest involves a variety of events including haybale stacking, fence jumping, pin the tail on the farmer, and of course, mooing. When the cows all stand in line for a particular event, they moo so loudly that the roar is practically deafening. After participating in this event year after year, some of the cows have in fact lost a bit of their hearing.

Each cow i has an associated “hearing” threshold v(i)v(i)v(i) (in the range 1…20,0001 \dots 20,0001…20,000). If a cow moos to cow iii, she must use a volume of at least v(i)v(i)v(i) times the distance between the two cows in order to be heard by cow iii. If two cows iii and jjj wish to converse, they must speak at a volume level equal to the distance between them times max(v(i),v(j))max(v(i),v(j))max(v(i),v(j)).

Suppose each of the NNN cows is standing in a straight line (each cow at some unique xxx coordinate in the range 1…20,0001 \dots 20,0001…20,000), and every pair of cows is carrying on a conversation using the smallest possible volume.

Compute the sum of all the volumes produced by all N×(N−1)2\frac{N \times (N-1)}{2}2N×(N−1)​ pairs of mooing cows.

INPUT FORMAT

Line 111: A single integer, NNN

Lines 2…N+12 \dots N+12…N+1: Two integers: the volume threshold and x coordinate for a cow. Line 222 represents the first cow; line 333 represents the second cow; and so on. No two cows will stand at the same location.

OUTPUT FORMAT

Line 111: A single line with a single integer that is the sum of all the volumes of the conversing cows

给定两个长度为 nnn 的序列 vi,xiv_i, x_ivi​,xi​， 求$$ \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} max(v_i, v_j) \times |x_i-x_j| (i \neq j)$$

题解

暴力枚举计算该式的复杂度为 O(n2)O(n^2)O(n2), 在原题的数据范围和现在的评测机运算能力下是可以通过的，不过这道题还有更优的做法。

这是一个二维偏序问题，可以考虑使用CDQ分治的思想解决

分析原来的式子，发现计算该式的瓶颈在于 max(vi,vj)max(v_i, v_j)max(vi​,vj​) ，考虑将原序列按照 viv_ivi​ 的值进行升序排列，对第二维进行分治
